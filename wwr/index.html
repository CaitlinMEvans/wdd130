Why Every Father Needs to Go On a River Trip with their Daughter

And what she taught me on our trip together

At one point during our 5-day father-daughter adventure on Oregon’s wild and impossibly scenic Rogue River, my 10-year-old daughter, Katie, taught me the true value of connection with nature and family. Watching her fearlessly navigate rapids, her laughter echoing against canyon walls, reminded me of the simple joy of being fully present. On the quiet stretches, she shared her dreams, her worries, and her unfiltered view of the world—insights I rarely took time to hear in the rush of our everyday lives. By the end of our journey, I realized that this trip wasn’t just about teaching her survival skills or guiding her through nature. It was about her teaching me to slow down, listen, and see the world through her eyes. Together, we discovered that the river isn’t just a place; it’s an experience that deepens our bond and renews our sense of wonder. It’s a journey every father and daughter should share, even just once....
